Creamy Vegan Pasta

Description

This Creamy Vegan Broccoli and Mushroom Pasta is a delicious and low-acid option without tomato, lemon, or other acidic ingredients. Enjoy as a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (about 2 cups) whole wheat or gluten-free pasta
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms (button or cremini)
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast (for a cheesy flavor, optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil or parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Steam the broccoli florets until they're tender, then set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  4. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté for a few minutes until they're tender and browned.
  5. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  6. Pour in the almond milk and nutritional yeast. Stir to combine and heat through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Toss the cooked pasta in the creamy mushroom sauce along with the steamed broccoli.
  9. Garnish with chopped fresh basil or parsley for added flavor and color.
