Tofu and Vegetable Stir Fry

Description

This dish presents a colorful and vibrant mix of stir-fried ingredients. The stir-fried tofu, a plant-based protein source, is usually cubed and lightly browned to a golden hue. It has a tender and slightly crispy texture on the outside, with a soft and creamy interior.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of extra-firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 cups mixed stir-fry vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas)
  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce or tamari
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (optional)
  • Cooked brown rice or quinoa for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat the sesame oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the tofu cubes and stir-fry until they are golden brown.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables, ginger, and garlic. Stir-fry for a few more minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the soy sauce and stir well to combine.
  5. Serve the stir-fry over cooked brown rice or quinoa.
