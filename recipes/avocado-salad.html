Avocado and Black Bean Salad

Description

The avocado provides a creamy, melt-in-the-mouth sensation, while the fresh vegetables and dressing add brightness and contrast. It's a visually pleasing, nutritious, and versatile salad that can be served as a side dish, a light lunch, or a refreshing appetizer, making it a favorite among avocado lovers and those seeking a satisfying and healthy salad option.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, canned, or frozen)
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: diced tomatoes, jalape√±os, or bell peppers for added flavor and color

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the black beans, diced avocados, corn, and finely chopped red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice and olive oil.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad ingredients and toss gently to combine.
  4. Add fresh cilantro and any optional ingredients you like.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the Avocado and Black Bean Salad as a side dish or as a filling for lettuce wraps. Enjoy!
